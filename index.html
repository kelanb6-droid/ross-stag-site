Barcelona Tips

Currency

Euro (€). Cards accepted everywhere. ATMs plentiful.

Language

Catalan & Spanish. English widely spoken in tourist areas.

Transport

Metro, buses, taxis. TMB app for public transport.

Weather

May is mild, 15-25°C. Pack layers.

Barcelona Highlights

Sagrada Familia

The iconic unfinished basilica by Gaudí. A must-see architectural marvel.

Park Güell

Colorful mosaic park with stunning views of the city.

La Rambla

Bustling street with street performers, markets, and nightlife.

Camp Nou

Home of FC Barcelona. Tour the stadium if you're a football fan.

Barcelona Fun Facts

Gaudí's Legacy

Barcelona is home to 7 UNESCO World Heritage sites, mostly designed by Antoni Gaudí.

Football Passion

FC Barcelona has over 140,000 season ticket holders. Camp Nou is the largest stadium in Europe.

Beach City

Barcelona has 4.2km of beaches right in the city center. Barceloneta is the most famous.

Catalan Culture

Catalonia has its own language and traditions. Try some tapas and sangria!

Barcelona Nightlife Guide

Top Bars

Opium Barcelona: Rooftop cocktails with city views.
Punto BCN: Chic lounge in Eixample.
La Chapelle: Speakeasy vibes in Gothic Quarter.
Átame: Trendy spot with great music.

Shisha Bars

Aladdin Shisha Bar: Traditional hookah in Raval.
Smoke Barcelona: Modern lounge with flavors.
Hookah Lounge BCN: Relaxed atmosphere near Plaça Catalunya.
El Born Shisha: Cozy spot in El Born district.

Nightlife Tips

Bars open late (till 3-5am). Happy hour 6-9pm. Dress code casual. Use taxis or metro after dark. Stay hydrated!
